Admission Process

A step-by-step guide to enrolling your child at T.F.A

  1. Obtain and complete the admission form (online or at the school office).
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, medical record, passport photographs, etc.).
  3. Schedule and attend an interview/assessment for the child (where applicable).
  4. Receive admission offer letter if successful.
  5. Pay the acceptance fee and school fees as outlined in the admission letter.
  6. Complete registration and attend orientation program.
