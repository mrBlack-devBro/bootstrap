Meet Our Founder

The Visionary Behind The Fountain Academy

Mrs. Omolola Adeseyoju

Mrs. Adeseyoju is a passionate educator and visionary leader dedicated to creating a school where every child is empowered to thrive. With a strong belief in the Montessori philosophy, she has nurtured Divine Fountain into a place where learning goes beyond academics to embrace creativity, discipline, and character building.

Her mission has always been to provide an environment where children feel loved, valued, and confident to explore their world. Through her tireless commitment, DFMS has grown into a vibrant institution that inspires excellence and molds future leaders with integrity and compassion.

Her Vision

Mrs. Adeseyoju believes that every child has a unique potential waiting to be unlocked. Her vision is to raise confident, creative, and well-rounded individuals who can make positive contributions to society. She continues to inspire staff, students, and parents through her unwavering dedication to excellence in education.
