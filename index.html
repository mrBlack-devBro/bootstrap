Welcome to The Fountain Academy

At The Fountain Academy, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds in a safe, stimulating, and loving environment. Guided by the Montessori philosophy, we encourage children to explore, discover, and grow at their own pace, while building confidence, responsibility, and a lifelong love for learning. Our mission is to partner with families in shaping future leaders who are compassionate, creative, and well-grounded.

About the Founder

The Fountain Academy was founded by Mrs. Omolola Adeseyoju, a passionate educator with a deep commitment to nurturing young minds. Inspired by the Montessori philosophy, she envisioned a school where children are encouraged to explore, create, and learn at their own pace. With her dedication and vision, The Fountain Academy has grown into a thriving learning community that empowers children to become confident, independent, and compassionate individuals.

Why The Fountain Academy?

At The Fountain Academy (T.F.A), we believe every child is unique and full of potential. Our Montessori approach fosters independence, curiosity, and creativity, ensuring that children not only excel academically but also develop socially, emotionally, and morally.
