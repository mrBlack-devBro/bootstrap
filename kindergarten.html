Kindergarten Program

Preparing children for a smooth transition to primary school

About Our Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program equips children with essential academic and social skills. We emphasize literacy, numeracy, and character-building to ensure a confident start to formal education.

Program Highlights

  • Reading and writing readiness
  • Number recognition and problem-solving
  • Role play and storytelling
  • Physical coordination and motor skills
  • Values and social responsibility
  • Creative arts and performance

Get your child ready for primary school success!
